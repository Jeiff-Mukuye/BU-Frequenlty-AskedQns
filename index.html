  • How To Access BU E-Learning Plateform

    Connect to internet >> Activate your browser i .e. Chrome, Mozilla, Edge. >> Type “elearning.bugemauniv.ac.ug” in the browser URL bar >> Click search on your Mouse or press enter on your keyboard >> The browser will open the platform home page

  • How to sign up or create an account?

    Connect to internet >> Open your browser and type “elearning.bugemauniv.ac.ug” to log on to the platform >> Click on the red log in button on the top right Conner
    a) TO CREATE AN NEW ACCOUNT
    >> Click on the blue arrow below your menu bar >> Click create new account >> Follow the prompts >> Click on “create my new account”. >> Wait for approval from the virtual office
    b) TO SIGN IN
    Fill in the Username and Password you entered while creating your account

  • How to reset or change a password?

    Connect to internet >> Log on to the e-learning plateform >> Click “forgot your username or password?” >> Enter “username” or “e-mail address” >> A message will be sent on your email address >> Follow the prompts

  • How update or change your account settings.

    Log on to the plateform >> Click on your name on the red button >> On the red ribbon that appears, click on Preferences >> Depending on what you need to edit click on it and follow the prompts

  • How to navigate and use the TOP MENU.

    The menu bar (where you find HOME | COURSES | TIMETABLES | E-LIBRARY | APPLY NOW | SUPPORT | DOCUMENTS&REPORTS | GUIDLINES/FAQS)

    HOME

    BU E-Learning Home >> This takes you to the page that welcomes you to the E-learingn Plateform

    BU Official Home >> This links you to the Bugema University Website

    COURSES

    Categories >> Displays all courses by Bugema University in their categories

    Courses A-Z >> Displays courses in an alphabetical order

    TIMETABLES

    Here you will find downloadable files of the current semester timetables for both Exams and Classes

    E-LIBRARY

    This tab links you to the BU E-Library system

    APPLY NOW

    This tab links you to the online application page

    SUPPORT

    Contact Us >> Here is a form were you can write to us

    Technical Team >> Here you will find the techinical team contacts incase you need to contact us

    DOCUMENTS & SUPPORT

    Under here you will find documents/policies regarding the E-Learning Plateform and IT policy documents
